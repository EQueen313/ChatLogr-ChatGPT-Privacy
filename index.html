Privacy Policy for ChatLogr - ChatGPT

Effective Date: 11/5/2025

ChatLogr (“the extension”) is a Chrome extension that helps you automatically capture, organize, and export your ChatGPT interactions. This policy explains what the extension collects, how it uses that information, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

While you are using ChatGPT, the extension may capture:

  • Prompts you enter
  • Responses returned by ChatGPT (including images and URLs/links, and their counts)
  • Click-through activity when you follow a link provided in a ChatGPT response
  • Metadata about the conversation, such as:
    • Conversation ID
    • Conversation title
    • Timestamp and timezone
    • Subscription type
    • Other technical details needed to associate events with a specific session

2. How We Store Data

All captured information is stored locally in your browser using Chrome’s storage mechanisms. The extension does not send this data to external servers, third parties, or the developer by default.

3. Why We Need Permissions

The extension requests certain Chrome permissions to deliver its functionality:

  • webRequest / webNavigation – to detect when new ChatGPT content is loaded and when you click through links from ChatGPT.
  • storage / unlimitedStorage – to save your captured sessions locally so you can review or export them later.
  • downloads – to let you export your captured data to CSV or JSON files.
  • tabs – to associate captured content with the correct ChatGPT tab/session.
  • Host permissions (e.g. https://chatgpt.com/* and user-initiated destinations) – to read the ChatGPT page where the interaction occurs and to detect click-throughs.

These permissions are used solely to provide the features described above and not for advertising, tracking across unrelated sites, or selling data.

4. Data Retention & Control

Data stays in your browser until you remove it. You can clear the extension’s stored data or uninstall the extension at any time to remove all locally stored information.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

The extension does not sell, rent, or otherwise share your captured data with third parties. Any exports (CSV/JSON) are initiated by you and saved to your devices.

6. Security

Because data is stored locally, it is protected by your browser’s and operating system’s security. Please keep your device and browser up to date.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page.
